Dear user:

Due to maintenance work on the building's general transformers, we would like to inform you that our data center will be partial shut down.

We will take advantage of this partial shutdown to perform maintenance on various equipment in the room.

* Shutdown of non-essential services: March 1, 2025, at 6:00 p.m.
* Basic services will remain online (storage, cloud, network, and cooling).
* The compute nodes will be shut down so that the restart does not affect them, and the queues will be closed on Friday, October 24, so that all jobs are drained correctly.
* Estimated duration of the shutdown: 4 hours.
