\[English version]

Dear user:

Due to maintenance work on the building's general transformers, we would like to inform you that our data center will be partial shut down.

We will take advantage of this partial shutdown to perform updates and maintenance on various equipment in the room.

\- Shutdown of non-essential services: October 27, 2025, at 10:00 a.m.

\- Basic services will remain online (storage, cloud, network, and cooling).

\- The compute nodes will be shut down so that the restart does not affect them, and the queues will be closed on Friday, October 24, so that all jobs are drained correctly.

\- The shutdown will be used to rewire the switch that broke down a couple of months ago. 

\- Estimated duration of the shutdown: 4 hours.

You can check the status of our services at any time at \[1].

If you have any questions, please contact us at \[2].

We apologize for any inconvenience.

IFCA Advanced Computing Service Team
